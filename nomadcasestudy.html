Travel App

Nomad

Make your trip easier after pandemic

Overview

As we know, the COVID-19 has spread all over the world. This pandemic has changed the means of travel, and preferences of the travelers. Nomad is an app which helps people to find the best travel packages, and provide the latest news, and information about the covid-19 for the hotels and flights.

My Role

  • User Research in form of user interview and survey.
  • Sketching, Wireframing and Prototyping.
  • Usability Testing and Evaluation.

Tools

  • Miro
  • Figma
  • InVision

Problem

Users who were fond of traveling found it difficult to find the best deals on transportation and accommodation while also focusing on food options and outdoor activities in the vicinity. Traveller have concerns about getting the latest covid information & regulations for different destinations, flights, hotels and the restaurants.

Solution

To solve the users problem, I build an app that offers the users the best deals and also gives them all the relevant information about destination, nearby attractions, activities, food options etc. based on their interests and safety concerns, so that instead of going all over the internet, this becomes their one stop shop for all their travel research and they can plan a trip without wasting a lot of time and energy; and without any hesitation.
