Hi there, my name is Vishakha!

I am a UX/UI designer, passionate about creating user-friendly designs that change human lives for the better!

I was born and brought up in India. I completed my bachelors degree in Information Technology from Pune University in India. I worked in the Indian Software Industry for a couple of years and then moved to the bay area, California after getting married.

In US, I honed my skills in front end technology like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript etc. and worked for a startup as a web designer. I enjoy learning new technologies that help me stay up to date in my field.

I always knew that I was passionate about front end design. Hence I’m currently pursuing a Program at UC Berkeley with a major in UX/UI design and working part time for a startup as UX/UI designer.

When I’m not busy wireframing, sketching, and coding, you’ll find me doing crafts, painting, playing games, hiking, biking or traveling somewhere in search of some awesome view or mouth watering food.
